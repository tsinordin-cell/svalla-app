Svalla — Log Your Archipelago Adventures

Svalla is the app for sailors exploring the Stockholm Archipelago. Track GPS routes, discover 69 islands, and follow other sailors' adventures.

LOG YOUR TRIPS
Start GPS tracking with one tap. Svalla records your route automatically in the background, even with the screen off. Add photos, write about your trip, and rate it with the ⚓ system.

EXPLORE THE ARCHIPELAGO
69 islands with restaurants, docks, and natural harbors. Find new favorites and plan your next trip directly in the app.

FOLLOW SAILORS
See what others are logging in real time. Follow the sailors you like, like trips, and leave comments. Share your trip as a link to friends who don't have the app.

STATISTICS
How far have you sailed this year? Which islands have you visited? Svalla keeps track of your nautical miles, days on the water, and fastest passages.

BADGES AND RECORDS
Unlock badges for milestones — your first trip, 100 NM, all islands visited. Climb the leaderboard.
